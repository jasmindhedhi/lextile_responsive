About Us

Here at Lextile, we a​re manufacturing exceptional quality PVC Leather products. We believe in the importance of company diversification, quality and global expansion as we work to produce synthetic leather in an industry-leading facility in India. We consider "People" to be a crucial component of the infrastructure. Our team is made up of highly skilled technical experts with many years of PVC product manufacturing experience. Our mission is to supply outstanding coated fabrics, with a focus on PVC, to the global automotive, furniture, footwear, apparel, hospitality and many more sectors. Through ongoing strategies, we also work to increase organization performance and continuously monitor the value generation for customers, suppliers, employees, administrative bodies, and society. 

Our main goal and Unique value proposition are high premium quality, digitization in technology, our team of qualified engineers, international exports, constant innovation and market research, consistency in repeat orders, variation, diversity as well as custom designed research and development for reputed economy, adaptivity, accessibility, connectivity, customisable sampling, processing strength and potential, on-time delivery, long - term cooperative interaction with customers, satisfaction of customers, and service.
